Returning back to our Incredible India...

Air Baltic, Air France, and many other intercontinental flights operate from France to India.

Let us have a look at the top tips for finding cheap flights to India...
->High season is considered to be January, February and December. The cheapest month to fly from Paris is January.
->Morning departure is around 34% more expensive than an evening flight, on an average.
->There are some direct flights available from Paris to India with a variety of destinations to choose from. Some of the most popular destinations to fly to are New Delhi and Mumbai and both can be reached with direct flights. There are also some non-direct flight routes available that may be cheaper, even if they take longer. Flights to southern cities such as Chennai will require at least one stop over, and it is likely to be in Mumbai.
